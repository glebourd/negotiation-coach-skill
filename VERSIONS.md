# Version History

## v1 — Foundation (current)

**Thesis:** Give anyone the preparation framework, practice environment, and feedback loop that business school negotiation courses provide -- without the tuition.

### What shipped

**Core coaching system**
- 15 commands covering the full negotiation lifecycle: preparation, practice, execution support, analysis, and identity development
- Persistent session state (`negotiation_state.md`) for continuity across sessions
- Auto-generated negotiation history (`negotiation_history.md`) that builds a personal case study library over time
- Coaching meta-checks and failure mode awareness

**Preparation and planning**
- Full planning documents (BATNA, RP, aspiration, ZOPA, issue mapping, strategy)
- Deep BATNA analysis with improvement plans for weak alternatives
- Scoring systems for multi-issue negotiations
- MESO (Multiple Equivalent Simultaneous Offers) package construction
- Leverage identification across six categories
- Strategy building with exact scripts for opening, countering, holding, and closing

**Practice and simulation**
- Eight simulation formats (offer, salary, raise, contract, pushback, silence, close, cold)
- Progressive pressure layers that find and drill failure modes
- Real-time counteroffer coaching with multiple response options
- Silence drill protocol for the most common behavioral gap

**Analysis and learning**
- Transcript analysis with tactical breakdown and scoring
- Post-negotiation debrief with principle violation detection
- Five-dimension rubric (Preparation, Positioning, Flexibility, Composure, Outcome) calibrated to experience level
- Root cause taxonomy connecting low scores to specific coaching interventions
- Self-assessment calibration tracking

**Negotiation identity development**
- Style awareness (cooperative/assertive/analytical)
- Earned principles extracted from real experience
- Pattern awareness across completed negotiations
- Identity narration after 5+ negotiations

**Frameworks from business school negotiation curricula**
- BATNA/RP/ZOPA/Aspiration Point framework
- Integrative vs. distributive diagnosis (the most consequential strategic fork)
- Anchoring theory and concession discipline
- MESOs and scoring systems
- Information strategy and silence management
- Psychological readiness and pressure management

## v2 — Depth (planned)

**Thesis:** Deepen the coaching intelligence -- better pattern detection, more sophisticated simulation, and multi-party negotiation support.

### Planned features
- Multi-party negotiation coaching (coalitions, alliances, side deals)
- Negotiation style profiling with validated assessment dimensions
- Advanced simulation with counterparty personality models (aggressive, collaborative, evasive, analytical)
- Email/async negotiation coaching (drafting, timing, tone calibration)
- Cultural context module for cross-cultural negotiations
- Negotiation journal with structured reflection prompts

## v3 — Lifecycle (planned)

### Planned features
